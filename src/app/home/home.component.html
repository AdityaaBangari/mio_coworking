Mio Coworks
redefining your work aesthetics.

Mio Coworks, as a brand, is dedicated to cultivating a contemporary wabi-sabi ambiance within coworking spaces. Our purpose revolves around adapting to the unique needs of our customers and consistently augmenting our facilities to elevate their overall experience. Embracing the essence of Japandi style, Mio Coworks infuses just the right amount of flair to transform your work environment.

Where you’ll find us

Memberships to suit everyone. Whether you’re flying solo, working with a team, or just need a desk for the day to get that pitch finished, we’ve got you.

Studios

Looking for a space to get the team together? Whether you prefer somewhere that’s all yours and totally private, or an office that feels more open but still has fixed desks, our Private and Open Studios come in all shapes and sizes.

Desks

Our Desk Hop memberships are great for those who want to drop in and out of our shared office space whenever they please. And our Side Hustler suits those pulling the evening and weekend shifts. We’ve got proper desks and good lighting, and storage box for storing any desk-side essentials.

Day Passes

Need a break from working from home? Sick of setting up camp on your sofa? Come and work at ours – with premium Arabica coffee to supercharge your brain

How we like to do business. We’re here ‘cos we want to make working nicer for you, with more balance, less irritations, and great perks for everyone.
